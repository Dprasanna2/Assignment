How buiding Detroit Works

Do your research

Think about what you really want in a home and what work you're willing to put into it. At BuildingDetroit.org, you'll find a photo gallery and a full condition report for each house up for auction. You can also attend an Open House to tour properties in person and get a clear understanding of the renovations required.

Bid and Win

Create an account on BuildingDetroit.org and bid on the on the house you love. Follow the auction closely so you can keep track of other bids. When you win your auction, you're a new homeowner.

Secure Financing

You may be eligible for financial assistance through one of our partners. Visit BuildingDetroit.org/Financing for more information.

Fix Up Your House

You don't have todo it all by yourself! The DLBA can refer you to trustworthy folks With a proven track record in your neighborhood Who Can help get your house up to code.

Share Your Progress

We'd love to know how it's going! Post updates and share stories on social media With *BuildingDetroit, or send us an email at email@buildinedetroit.org. Let'S Show What Detroit Can do!

Be Part of the Community

Once you're all moved in. help other new neighbors by sharing your experiences and informing them about incentives and Other resources available. Let'S make our neigh borhoods strong together.
